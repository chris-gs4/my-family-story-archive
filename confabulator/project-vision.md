# Project Vision: Mabel

## Vision Statement
We are building Mabel, an AI memoir companion that helps people capture and preserve their life stories through guided voice interviews. Like a skilled biographer in your pocket, Mabel interviews you chapter by chapter — asking thoughtful questions that draw out your stories and help you re-access memories you'd forgotten. Our module-based system lets users build their memoir one chapter at a time, with a warm, pixel-art grandmother character named Mabel as their companion throughout. Designed for anyone who wants to preserve their personal history — and for anyone who wants to preserve a loved one's stories before they're lost. We aim to create a world where everyone's story is told, preserved, and cherished — no writing required, just talking.

## The Problem We're Solving
Everyone has a life story worth preserving, but very few people ever write it down. The idea of "writing a book" feels overwhelming, time-consuming, and reserved for professional writers. Most people don't know where to start, and the blank page is paralyzing. Meanwhile, precious memories — childhood adventures, pivotal life moments, lessons learned — fade with time. Think about your grandparents. The stories they carry that you've never heard. The ones that will disappear when they do. The cost of inaction is a lost personal legacy, memories that could have inspired future generations left uncaptured.

## Our Solution
Mabel reframes story preservation from "writing a book" into something anyone can do: **guided voice interviews with a friendly AI memoir companion**. Instead of facing a blank page, users simply talk to Mabel. She interviews you chapter by chapter — asking the right questions, drawing out your stories the way a skilled biographer would, and transforming your spoken memories into polished written narratives. You speak your autobiography into existence.

**Key Innovation: Context-Learning AI**
Mabel doesn't just ask generic questions — she learns. Module 1 establishes the foundation based on your life profile. Module 2 analyzes those responses to generate smarter follow-up questions. By Module 3, Mabel is weaving connections between themes, diving deeper into interesting topics, and helping you rediscover memories you hadn't thought about in years.

**The Module Advantage:**
- **Prevents overwhelm:** Complete bite-sized chapters instead of facing a massive writing project
- **Flexible pacing:** Work over days, weeks, or months — your choice
- **Better narratives:** Each chapter builds on previous ones
- **Natural structure:** Modules become chapters in your story
- **Higher completion rates:** Smaller commitments mean people actually finish

**The Mabel Experience:**
- **Voice-first:** Just tap record and speak — no writing required, talking is the UX
- **Guided interviews:** Mabel asks the questions a skilled biographer would, drawing out stories you'd forgotten
- **Interactive companion:** Mabel reacts to your stories with warmth and encouragement
- **Effortless memoir:** Writing a memoir feels daunting — talking to Mabel doesn't

Now is the perfect time for this innovation, as advances in AI language models enable truly context-aware conversations that understand and build upon previous interactions.

## Target Users
**Storytellers:** Anyone who wants to capture their own life story — whether motivated by personal reflection, milestone birthdays, a desire to leave something meaningful behind, or simply the joy of revisiting old memories. These users span all ages and backgrounds but share a common desire: to preserve their experiences in a lasting, beautiful way. Because talking is the UX, anyone can use Mabel — including your grandparents.

**Gift givers:** People who want to preserve a loved one's stories before they're lost. Think about your grandparents — the stories they carry that you've never heard, the ones that will disappear when they do. Mabel makes it almost effortless to capture them. Gift Mabel via the Family Plan so loved ones can speak their memoir into existence.

## Value Proposition
Your memoir companion. Just talk — Mabel writes. Our voice-first approach removes the barrier of writing, our module system eliminates overwhelm, and our context-learning AI interviews you like a skilled biographer — creating richer narratives than you'd ever produce on your own. We stand out with:
- **No writing required:** Just speak naturally — talking is the UX, so anyone can use it
- **Skilled AI interviewer:** Questions that learn and adapt, drawing out stories you'd forgotten
- **Chapter-by-chapter structure:** Build your memoir gradually, never feel overwhelmed
- **Professional results:** AI-generated chapters that read like a professionally written memoir
- **Complete control:** Regenerate chapters with feedback, approve when perfect

Users choose Mabel because it turns the daunting idea of "writing a memoir" into something effortless — and produces a priceless legacy in a beautifully written story.

## Long-Term Vision
In the next 3-5 years, Mabel aspires to be the go-to platform for personal memoir creation. We aim to evolve from individual story capture to a complete memory ecosystem — with family plans, audiobooks narrated in your own voice, photo integration, and multi-language support.

**Progress Milestones:**
- **Phase 1 (Current):** MVP launch with voice-first guided memoir interviews, AI narrative generation, PDF export
- **Phase 2:** Family Plan (gift Mabel, shared family archive), gamification features (streaks, milestones, surprise prompts), standard audiobook narration
- **Phase 3:** Voice cloning (hear your story in your own voice), collaborative family editing, photo/artifact integration
- **Phase 4:** Multi-language support, Android app, partnerships with wellness and legacy platforms, physical book printing

Our impact will be seen in how people reconnect with their own histories — rediscovering forgotten memories, finding new meaning in their experiences, and creating something lasting for the people they love.

## Guiding Principles
1. **Authenticity Above All**: We prioritize preserving genuine voices and stories, ensuring each narrative is true to the storyteller's essence.
2. **Fun Over Formal**: Writing a memoir sounds daunting. Talking to Mabel doesn't. The experience should feel like chatting with a friend who genuinely wants to hear your story.
3. **User-Centric Design**: Our platform is intuitive and accessible, designed to make storytelling effortless and enjoyable.
4. **Emotional Resonance**: We focus on creating products that evoke emotion, understanding that the value lies in the personal connection and impact of the stories told.
5. **Privacy and Respect**: We are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our users, treating their stories with the utmost respect and confidentiality.
