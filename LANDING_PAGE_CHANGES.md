# Landing Page Redesign - Journaling-First Positioning

## Overview

The new landing page shifts from a **sales-focused memoir tool** to an **educational journaling app** explanation page. It's less about "selling Mabel" and more about explaining what it is, how it works, and why it's different.

---

## Key Changes

### 1. **New Tagline**
**Old:** "Your Stories, Written with Care"
**New:** "Journal Freely. Share Beautifully."

**Why:** Emphasizes the journaling-first approach and the transformation from raw → polished.

---

### 2. **Section Structure (Completely Reorganized)**

| Old Landing Page | New Landing Page |
|------------------|------------------|
| Hero (solution-first) | Hero (simple, clean introduction) |
| How It Works (3 steps) | **What is Mabel?** (problem → solution explanation) |
| What Makes Mabel Different | How It Works (3 steps + habit building) |
| Gift of a Lifetime (Family Plan) | **Mabel vs StoryWorth** (comparison table) |
| The Vision (roadmap) | **Pricing** (Free → Premium → Add-ons) |
| Your Story Deserves to Be Told | Start Journaling Today (CTA) |
| Tech Stack | Tech Stack |
| Footer | Footer |

**Removed:**
- "Gift of a Lifetime" section (not core to MVP messaging)
- "The Vision" roadmap (too forward-looking for an explanatory page)
- "Your Story Deserves to Be Told" emotional close (redundant with new CTA)

**Added:**
- "What is Mabel?" section (educational foundation)
- "Mabel vs StoryWorth" comparison table (clear differentiation)
- "Pricing" section (transparency, free tier emphasis)

---

### 3. **Section-by-Section Breakdown**

#### **Hero Section**
**Old:**
> "Everyone has a story worth preserving, but writing is intimidating. Mabel removes the blank page. Just talk — she listens, transcribes, and transforms your spoken memories into beautifully written chapters..."

**New:**
> "Mabel
> Journal Freely. Share Beautifully.
> A journaling app that transforms your raw thoughts into polished narratives"

**Why:** Simpler, cleaner. Removes the "memoir" framing and positions Mabel as a journaling tool.

---

#### **What is Mabel? (NEW SECTION)**
**Content:**
- Problem: "Everyone has stories worth sharing, but raw journal entries are messy and often unpublishable. So they sit in Notes apps, gathering digital dust."
- Solution: "Mabel is a journaling tool that transforms your raw thoughts into polished narrative."
- Differentiation: "Unlike traditional memoir services that ask you to commit to a year-long writing project, Mabel lets you capture life as it happens."
- Output: "Over time, your entries become chapters. Your chapters become a story. And when you're ready, you can export a PDF book or order a fully narrated audiobook."

**Why:** Sets the foundation. Explains the problem, solution, and unique approach in plain language.

---

#### **How It Works**
**Old:**
- 3 steps: Speak → Mabel writes → Get your book
- Focus: Memoir creation

**New:**
- 3 steps: Capture your thoughts → AI polishes → Build your story
- **Added:** "Building the Journaling Habit" callout box
  - "Mabel includes gentle reminders and gamified features (streaks, milestones, chapter completion) to help you develop a consistent journaling practice. The goal isn't to finish a book in a year—it's to capture life as you live it."

**Why:** Reframes from "book creation" to "journaling habit." Explicitly mentions gamification features you plan to build.

---

#### **Mabel vs StoryWorth (NEW SECTION)**
**Content:** Full comparison table

| Feature | Mabel | StoryWorth |
|---------|-------|------------|
| Mental Model | Journaling habit | Memoir project |
| Time Commitment | Journal anytime | 52 weeks, 1 prompt/week |
| Input Method | Voice-first (or text) | Email prompts → typing |
| AI Polish | Narrative transformation | Basic formatting |
| Pace | Your schedule | Weekly deadline |
| Output | Export anytime (PDF/audiobook) | Hardcover book after 1 year |
| Best For | Active journalers & beginners | Family gift projects |

**Closing statement:**
> "In short: StoryWorth says 'write your memoir.' Mabel says 'journal your life, and let AI polish it into something shareable.' Journaling is a daily habit. Memoir writing is a daunting project. We chose the former."

**Why:** Makes the differentiation crystal clear. Shows you've thought about the competition and have a distinct positioning.

---

#### **Pricing (NEW SECTION)**
**Structure:** 3-column layout

**Free Tier:**
- $0
- First chapter free
- Voice & text journaling
- AI narrative polish
- PDF export

**Premium Tier (RECOMMENDED):**
- $9.99/month or $79.99/year
- Unlimited chapters
- All features
- Gamification
- Private & secure

**Add-Ons (Coming Soon):**
- Narrated Audiobook: $29.99 (your story, in your voice, AI cloned)
- Printed Hardcover: $49.99+ (physical book)
- Note: "Available after 10+ chapters"

**Why:** Transparency builds trust. Shows you have a clear business model. Free tier lowers barrier to entry.

---

#### **Start Journaling Today (CTA)**
**Old:** Multiple CTAs scattered throughout, ending with emotional "Your Story Deserves to Be Told"

**New:** Single, focused CTA section
> "Your stories deserve to be told. Mabel makes it effortless to capture them, polish them, and share them—one journal entry at a time."
> [Try Mabel Free] + QR Code

**Why:** Clean, simple call to action. No pressure, just invitation.

---

## Design Changes

### Color Palette
**Old:** Heavy use of gradient backgrounds (teal → cream throughout)

**New:**
- Hero: Teal gradient (kept for warmth)
- Body sections: Clean white or subtle cream (#FAF8F3)
- Emphasis: Teal (#2F6F5E) for highlights only

**Why:** More professional, easier to read, less "whimsical."

---

### Typography
**Kept:** Georgia serif for headers, system sans-serif for body

**Changed:** Reduced font sizes slightly for better readability on long-form content

---

### Layout
**Old:** Marketing-style sections with icons, emojis, cards

**New:**
- More text-heavy, editorial style
- Table layout for comparison section
- Clean pricing cards
- Less visual noise overall

**Why:** Feels more like a product explanation page, less like a landing page trying to "sell" you.

---

## What's Removed (and Why)

### ❌ "Gift of a Lifetime" (Family Plan)
**Reason:** Not part of MVP. Distracts from core journaling message. Can add back later when Family Plan is ready.

### ❌ "The Vision" (Roadmap)
**Reason:** Too forward-looking for an explanatory page. Users want to know what it does NOW, not in Phase 3. Roadmap is better suited for a blog post or About page.

### ❌ "What Makes Mabel Different" (4 feature cards)
**Reason:** Replaced by the more direct "Mabel vs StoryWorth" comparison table. Table is clearer and more actionable.

---

## What's Added (and Why)

### ✅ "What is Mabel?" (Foundational explanation)
**Reason:** Users need to understand the problem/solution before diving into features. This sets the stage.

### ✅ "Mabel vs StoryWorth" (Comparison table)
**Reason:** Clear, scannable differentiation. Answers the #1 question: "How is this different from StoryWorth?"

### ✅ "Pricing" (3-tier pricing table)
**Reason:** Transparency builds trust. Free tier lowers barrier. Shows you have a sustainable business model.

---

- **Problem:** Memories fade, writing is intimidating

## For Demo Night: Key Talking Points

### Opening (1 minute)
> "I've been journaling for 7 years. I have hundreds of entries scattered across my Notes app. I don't know what to do with them. That's the problem Mabel solves.
>
> Raw journal entries are messy. They're not shareable. Mabel is a journaling app that transforms your raw thoughts into polished narrative—automatically. You just speak or type, and AI does the polishing in the background.
>
> Over time, your entries become chapters. Your chapters become a story. When you're ready, you can export a PDF book or order a narrated audiobook.
>
> StoryWorth asks you to commit to 52 weeks of memoir writing. Mabel asks you to just... journal. Whenever inspiration strikes. No prompts, no pressure, no homework.
>
> That's the difference: journaling is a daily habit. Memoir writing is a daunting project. We chose the former."
